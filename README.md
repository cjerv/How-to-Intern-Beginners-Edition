## My First Coin


Chinese New Year 2020, was when I first realized that my aviation career might be derailed, more specifically January 27th.  Prior to heading off to Singapore, Bangkok, and Singapore, for a 4 night 3 day all expenses paid tour of SE Asia, I recall my wife handing me face masks and insisting that I wear them when walking though the busy airport terminals. I choose to politely thanks her, put the masks in my pocket and forget about them. Once we got into the terminal and mixed with many coughing transit passengers heading to Singapore for the holiday, I quickly change my thinking and put on the mask.

Shortly after Chinese New Year, Hong Kong people stopped travelling due to memories of SARS, resulting in Cathay and Dragonair parking aircraft and most pilots and cabin crew being grounded for over one year.

While beginning to wear out my Xbox controller playing COD, I also began devoting more time to investing properly, trying to learn how to trade futures [(Thanks Sven)][df6], trying to learn Cantonese, and consuming [Real Vision][df5] content.

My good friend on the same investing journey suggested we buy some bitcoin from a local OTC exchange.  I decided to follow his lead and got my first chunk of BTC in the $6k range.  Thanks Sean!

Having zero coding experience (Grade 9 BASIC doesn't count), I had to figure out a way that would hopefully make you consider advancing my application. So here we are!

After spending some time to consider my options and realizing that there would be very little chance for me to learn Python by Saturday, then be skilled enough with my new-found language to detect an issue and contribute some new code I felt I would focus on the challenge presented and my non-programming skills so I reverted my attention to the Role Discription where I have many more transferrable skills.
